What we do

Our strategic initiatives drive our work and deliver tangible improvements for all users of Ethereum.

Scale

→ Ethereum will provide enough blockspace on L1 to accommodate all asset issuance, governance, DeFi and L2 settlement activity, without compromising on hardness. → August 2025: Protocol Update 001

Improve UX

→ Seamless, secure and permissionless experience across the Ethereum ecosystem, for individuals and institutions. → August 2025: Protocol Update 003

Harden the L1

→ Ethereum will retain the properties — security, censorship resistance, network resilience — that make it valuable in the first place as it scales.
